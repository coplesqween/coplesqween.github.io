1. Preparation Before Updating#

The very first step before updating your Samsung Galaxy S8 to Android 11 is to back up all your data. This includes your contacts, music, messages, apps, and other important information stored on your phone. To back up your data, simply go to your settings and select ‘Backup and Reset.’ Once your data is backed up, you’re ready to proceed to the next step.

The second step before downloading Android 11 for your Galaxy S8 is to free up some space on your device. This will ensure that the new OS will be downloaded fast and that it will run smoothly without any problems. To free up space, delete any apps and files that you no longer use.

The third step is to download the latest version of Android and the necessary updates from Samsung’s website. Head to their official website and look for the ‘Download Center.’ Once the download is complete, transfer the files to your phone.

Finally, once all the preparations are made, you can now begin updating your Samsung Galaxy S8 to the latest version of Android. Make sure to read the instructions carefully before proceeding with the update. This will ensure that the process is completed successfully and without any issues.
