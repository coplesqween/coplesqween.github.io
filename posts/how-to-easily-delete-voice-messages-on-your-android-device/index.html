Voice messages are one of the most convenient ways to communicate with your friends and family since they don’t require any typing and can be sent quickly and easily. Android users now have the ability to send and receive voice messages from their Android devices. However, it’s also important to know how to delete them whenever you need to. Here’s a simple guide on how to easily delete voice messages on your Android device.

First, open the Messages app on your device. If you already have your conversation list open, you can simply swipe left on the message you want to delete. You should see a Trash icon appear next to it. Tap that to delete the message.

If you don’t have the conversation list open, you can still delete messages. You can simply tap the conversation you want to delete messages from, and then tap the Trash icon in the top-right corner of the screen. This should open a window with all the messages in the conversation. Check the messages you want to delete and then tap the Trash icon in the bottom-right corner of the window.

You can also delete messages from the home screen of your device. All you have to do is hold down on the message you want to delete until the message expands. Then, tap the Trash icon in the top-right corner of the expanded message.

Finally, you can delete all messages from a conversation at once. To do this, tap the conversation to open it and then tap the three dots icon in the top-right corner. Tap the Delete conversation option, and the entire conversation, including all the messages, will be deleted.

As you can see, deleting voice messages on Android is easy and straightforward. Once you know the steps, you can quickly delete any message you don’t need anymore.
