1. Introduction to WhatsApp Voice Messages#

Saving WhatsApp voice messages on your Android phone can be a great way to preserve memories and save important conversations for reference. WhatsApp makes it easy to record and send voice messages, but sometimes you may need to save those voice messages for later. Luckily, Android offers a few different ways to quickly and easily save your WhatsApp voice messages. Here are the steps to save voice messages on Android.

First, open the WhatsApp conversation containing the voice message you want to save. Long press on the message and then select the ‘Save to Gallery’ option. Your voice message will be immediately saved in the WhatsApp directory.

Second, go to your phone’s file manager and find the WhatsApp folder. All of your WhatsApp videos and images are stored in the WhatsApp folder. You can then either copy the voice message and transfer it to your desired location, or simply long press on the voice message and select the ‘Move’ option.

Third, you can also directly share the voice message with yourself via email or another social platform. Simply long press on the voice message, hit the share button, and choose the appropriate option. The voice message will then be stored in the downloads folder or the application you chose for the sharing option.

Finally, you can use apps like ‘Audio Manager’ or ‘Voice Recorder’ to save WhatsApp voice messages. Install the apps on your Android phone and then follow the directions to easily save and store voice messages.

These are just a few of the ways to quickly and easily save WhatsApp voice messages on Android. With a few simple steps, you can easily save your voice messages and keep them safe and secure.

3. Accessing Saved Voice Messages#

WhatsApp voice messages are a great way to communicate with friends and family over the internet. They are easy to send and receive, and can be saved on your Android device. Here’s a quick guide on how to save Whatsapp voice messages on Android.

First, open WhatsApp and find the voice message that you would like to save. Tap on the message and then tap on the three vertical dots at the top right of the screen. This will open a drop-down menu. Tap on ‘Save to Gallery’ in the menu. This will save the voice message to your phone’s memory.

Alternatively, you can also press and hold the voice message until the following menu appears. This will give you the option to ‘Save to Gallery’. Tap on the option to save the voice message.

Finally, you can check if the voice message was successfully saved by opening the Gallery 10 or Photos app on your Android device. The voice message should appear in the list of photos and videos.

With these steps, you can easily save voice messages on WhatsApp. You can then listen to them any time and share them with your friends and family.

Saving your WhatsApp voice messages on Android device can be done quickly and easily with a few simple steps. By following these simple steps, you can easily save any WhatsApp voice messages on Android.

II. Transfer Voice Messages to App#

Android phones come with a built-in WhatsApp Messenger function allowing users to send and receive text, photos and voice messages. Unfortunately, these voice messages are not stored on the phone and will be deleted once they are played. Here’s a step-by-step guide on how you can save WhatsApp voice messages on your Android phone. First, open the WhatsApp Messenger app on your Android phone. Go to the conversation with the voice message you want to save and tap the audio message. On the next screen, tap the “More options” icon (three vertical dots) and select “Save to Audio”. This will save the voice message in the WhatsApp audio folder in your phone’s memory. Second, connect your phone to your computer with the USB cable. Go to the “My Computer” icon and select “Removable Device”. Open the WhatsApp audio folder and copy the desired voice message to your computer. Third, if you want to store the voice message in a cloud service, move the audio file to the Cloud Storage folder. To do this, right-click the file and select “Copy”. Then, open the Cloud Storage folder, right-click and select “Paste”. Finally, store the voice message in the desired folder. You can also rename the file for easy access. That is all it takes to save WhatsApp voice messages on your Android phone.
