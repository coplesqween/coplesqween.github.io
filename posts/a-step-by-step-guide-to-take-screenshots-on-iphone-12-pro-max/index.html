1.Understanding the Different Ways of Taking Screenshots#

Taking a screenshot on iPhone 12 Pro Max is a breeze. There are a few different ways to capture the screen of your beloved iPhone 12 Pro Max. The most popular way is to use the combination of the Sleep/Wake button and Volume Up button. First, press and hold the Sleep/Wake button and then quickly press and release the Volume Up button. You should see your iPhone 12 Pro Max’s display blink quickly and you should then hear the sound of the camera shutter. Your screenshot will be automatically saved to your Photos app.

Another way of taking a screenshot is by using the Assistive Touch feature on your iPhone 12 Pro Max. To use this feature, first, go to your iPhone 12 Pro Max’s Settings app and select General. Then, select Accessibility and toggle AssistiveTouch on. To take a screenshot, press the AssistiveTouch icon and then select the ‘Device’ tab and then hit ‘More’. Finally, select ‘Screenshot’ and you should see your display blink and the screenshot be saved automatically to your Photos app.

Finally, you can take a screenshot on your iPhone 12 Pro Max using a Mac. To do this, connect your iPhone 12 Pro Max to your Mac via USB and open the QuickTime Player app. Then, select ‘File’ followed by ‘New Movie Recording’. Once you have done that, click on the drop-down menu beside the record button and select your iPhone 12 Pro Max. You should now be able to see your iPhone 12 Pro Max’s display on your Mac. To take the screenshot, click on the record button and you should hear the sound of the camera shutter. Your screenshot should be saved automatically to your Mac’s Pictures folder.

There you have it, three different ways to take screenshots on your iPhone 12 Pro Max. With this step-by-step guide, you now know just how easy it is to capture everything on your iPhone 12 Pro Max’s display.
