1. What You Need to Connect AirPods Pro with Android Devices#

AirPods Pro are one of the hottest accessories on the market right now and many Android users are interested in pairing their device with these popular wireless earbuds. With the right setup, you’ll be able to enjoy all the features of your AirPods Pro with your Android device.
