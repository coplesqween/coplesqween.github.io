Transferring files between two Android devices can be a simple task if you know how to do it. First of all, you need to make sure both devices have Bluetooth enabled. Here are the steps to transfer all your files from one Android device to another:

  1. On the source device, open the Settings app and tap on the Connection & Sharing option.
  2. Tap on the Bluetooth option and make sure the toggle is enabled.
  3. On the destination device, enable the Bluetooth in the same way.
  4. Pair the two devices by tapping on the device name.
  5. On the source device, select the files or folders you want to transfer and tap the Share button.
  6. Select the destination device from the nearby devices list and send the files.
  7. The files will then start transferring over Bluetooth.
  8. Once the files are transferred, you can access them on the destination device.
