Q2: How do I use the Move to iOS app?

A2: To transfer your WhatsApp chats with the Move to iOS app, first download the Move to iOS app on your Android phone. Then turn on your iPhone and follow the onscreen instructions until you get to the Apps & Data screen. On your Android device, open the Move to iOS app and tap Continue. Then agree to the terms and conditions. Tap Continue on your iPhone. On your Android device, enter the 10-digit code shown on your iPhone. Then select the data you’d like to transfer, including WhatsApp chats. When the transfer is complete, tap Done on your Android device.

Q3: How do I back up my WhatsApp chats?

A3: To back up your WhatsApp chat history, open the WhatsApp app on your Android device. Go to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup, and tap Back Up. Connect your iPhone to the computer and open iTunes. Select your iPhone under DEVICES in the left pane, and then select the Apps tab. Scroll to the bottom and select File Sharing. Select your WhatsApp app, and select the Backups folder. Copy the backup file to the desktop.

Q4: How do I restore my WhatsApp chats?

A4: On your iPhone, open the WhatsApp app and tap Restore. Then locate the backup file you created from your Android device and select it. Select Restore to restore your WhatsApp chat history.
