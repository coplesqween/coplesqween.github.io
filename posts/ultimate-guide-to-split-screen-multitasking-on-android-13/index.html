1.What is Split-Screen Multitasking#

Split-screen multitasking is a feature on Android 13 that allows you to open two or more apps at the same time and view them side-by-side. To get the most out of this feature, it’s important to know how to use it properly. For starters, you’ll need to open the application that you want to multitask with, then pull up the multitasking menu by tapping the square icon at the bottom of the screen. From there, you can select the app you want to open in the other window. Once both apps are open, you can adjust the size of each window to suit your needs. Finally, to switch back to single app mode, simply close the app you no longer want to use in split-screen mode. With split-screen multitasking, you can quickly switch between multiple apps at once, making it easier to get more work done.

2. How to Do Split Screen in Android 13#

Split-screen multitasking is a feature many Android users are already familiar with. It allows two apps to run physically side by side on the same screen, making it easy to copy and paste text between apps, play a game while keeping an eye on your conversations, and more. Android 13 offers an improved version of the feature that makes it even easier to get the most out of your device. Here’s how to do split-screen multitasking with Android 13. First, open the Recent Apps page by swiping up from the bottom of the home screen to display all of your recently used programs. Tap and hold an app thumbnail to select it for split-screen multitasking. Then select the second app you want to run either from the Recent list or from the app drawer. The two apps will appear side-by-side, giving you the ability to control each one independently. You can adjust the size of the apps by dragging the center divider left and right. When you’re done using split-screen, simply drag the center divider to the bottom of the screen to exit split-screen mode.

Q5: How do I exit split-screen multitasking? A5: To exit split-screen multitasking, you can either press the recent apps button or press and hold the app icon on either of the apps displayed in split-screen and drag it to the X icon in the top corner of your device.
