A. Establishing Bluetooth Connection#

Pairing wireless AirPods with your TCL Android TV is a great option if you want to enjoy the convenience and freedom of wireless audio. To connect your AirPods to your TV, your TV must support Android 5.0 or later. Here’s how to do it:

First, ensure that your AirPods are charged and within 8-10 feet of your TV. Then, press the Home button on your TV remote control and select Settings. Once in Settings, select Network and Internet. Then select Bluetooth and then click on Pair a New Device. Your TV will now search for the AirPods.

Once the AirPods appear on the list of devices, select them and the TV will begin pairing. It may take several seconds for the process to complete. Once connected, your AirPods will be ready for use with your TCL Android TV.

If your AirPods don’t appear on the list of devices, try resetting them and then repeat the above steps. Additionally, you can try moving the AirPods closer to your TV or even replace the battery of your AirPods if needed.

To disconnect your AirPods from your TV, simply return to the Bluetooth menu in the Settings option and select the Forget Device option. This will remove the AirPods from the list of paired devices. Pairing AirPods to your TCL Android TV is a great way to enjoy your favourite content wirelessly.

3. How to Disconnect AirPods from TCL Android TV#

Disconnecting AirPods from your TCL Android TV is easy and can be achieved within a few steps. All you need to do is un-pair the AirPods from the TV and the connection will be terminated. Here are the steps to follow:

Go to your TV’s home screen and select Settings. Under Bluetooth section, all connected AirPods should be listed. Select the AirPods you want to disconnect and choose the Unpair or Forget option. You will be asked to confirm if you want to unpair the AirPods from the TV. Select Yes and the pairing will be disconnected.

The next time you want to connect the AirPods to the TV, you need to pair them again by following the usual steps. Ensure that your AirPods have enough battery life before you connect them to the TV as the connection will be ended in case of low battery.

Finally, it is recommended to keep your TV’s software up-to-date to avoid any potential pairing problems. Regularly check the system updates available for your TCL Android TV and download as soon as possible. This will ensure that your AirPods work perfectly with the TV without any pairing issues.

2. Establish Connection to TCL Android TV#

One of the great features available on TCL Android TVs is connection to Apple AirPods.
