Step 1: Use the Print Screen button

The Print Screen button on your keyboard is the most convenient way to take screenshots on a Lenovo laptop with Windows 10. This button is usually labelled “PrtSc”, “PrtScn” or “Print Scr” and is usually found on the top-right of your keyboard. When you press this button, it will capture a snapshot of your entire screen and save it to the clipboard.

Step 2: Open Paint

Once you have pressed the Print Screen button, you will need to open up Paint in order to save the screenshot. To do this, click on the Windows button located on the bottom-left of your screen and type in “Paint”. Once Paint has opened up, click on the Paste button located on the Home tab.

Step 3: Save the Screenshot

Finally, click on the Save as button and select the location and type of file you wish to save your screenshot as. You can save your screenshot as a PNG, JPG, GIF, or even a PDF file. Once you have saved your screenshot, it is now ready for sharing or further editing.

By following these simple steps, you can easily take screenshots on your Windows 10 Lenovo laptop. Taking screenshots has never been easier and now you can conveniently capture everything you need with just the touch of a button.
