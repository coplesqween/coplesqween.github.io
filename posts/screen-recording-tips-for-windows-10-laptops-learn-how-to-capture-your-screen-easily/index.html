Next, use the Game Bar’s control buttons to start the screen recording. You can use the “Start recording” button to begin capturing the screen. Additionally, you can adjust the audio recording settings and quality of the video before you begin.

Once you have finished recording, the Game Bar will automatically stop the recording and show an indicator on the screen.
