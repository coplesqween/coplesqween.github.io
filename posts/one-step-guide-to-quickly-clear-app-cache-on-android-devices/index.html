3. Step-by-Step Guide on How to Clear App Cache on Android Devices All at Once#

If you use Android mobile devices, you probably know that clearing application caches can help improve performance, free up storage space while ultimately improving device stability. However, you may find it time-consuming to clear all the cache files one by one from each of your apps. Fortunately, there is a one-step way to clear app caches on your Android device all at once. Here’s how you can clear all the application caches at once.

Go to Settings > Storage & USB > Cached Data and tap on it. This will bring up a pop-up window asking you to confirm the clear all application caches action. Tap on the “OK” button to complete the action. All your application caches will be cleared from your device instantly.

It’s important to note that this action will not delete any of your app data or files, it will only clear the application caches. Clearing the app cache on an Android device can help you free up storage space on your device as well as solve any possible application issues.
