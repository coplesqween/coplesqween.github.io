1. How to Take a Screenshot on Windows 11 HP Envy#

Taking screenshots on Windows 11 HP Envy can be a valuable skill for multiple purposes, such as recording high scores for an online game or quickly sharing something interesting with friends. Whether you’re just starting out with Windows 11 HP Envy or have been a long-time user, you can quickly master the art of taking screenshots with the below instructions.

First, press the ‘Windows’ button on your keyboard, and ‘PrtScn’ (or ‘PrtSc’) button. This will take a screenshot of your entire screen and save it as a file on your PC. Alternatively, you can also use the combination of ‘Windows + Shift + S’ to enter a screenshot mode. After that, select the part of your screen you’d like to capture and copy the image to the clipboard.

Second, if you’d like to take a screenshot of your entire screen and save it as an image, press the ‘PrtScn’ (or ‘PrtSc’) button. Go to the ‘Photos’ app and select ‘Screenshots’ from the left pane. Now you have your screenshot in the Photos section and you can edit, share, or delete it as you like.

Finally, if you’d like to capture an active window, press the ‘Alt + PrtScn’ button. This will take a screenshot of the active window and copy it to the clipboard. You can paste the image into your favorite image editor for further editing, such as cropping or resizing.

With these simple steps, you can now easily master the art of taking a screenshot on Windows 11 HP Envy. Now get out there and start snapping!
