If you’re a MacBook Air M2 user, chances are you’ve wanted to take a screenshot at some point. Taking screenshots on your laptop isn’t as difficult as it seems. Here are 5 easy steps to help you get your screen captured within minutes.

First, you need to locate the ‘Command’ and ‘Shift’ keys on your keyboard. These two keys are what will be used to help you capture your screen. Once you’ve found them, press both simultaneously, then press the ‘4’ key. This will bring up a selection box that you can move around with your cursor. Use this to select the area of the screen you want to capture.

Once you’ve highlighted the area you want, release the keys and a thumbnail of the screenshot will appear in the bottom right-hand corner of your screen. Don’t worry if you’ve made a mistake; this thumbnail is purely a preview and can be discarded without saving it.

Next, if you’d like to save your screenshot, you should press the ‘Spacebar’. Doing this will bring up a program called ‘Preview’ where you can crop and edit the image. When you’re happy with the image, simply press ‘File’, then select ‘Save As’ to store the screenshot in your documents.

Finally, if you’d like to copy the screenshot to the clipboard, press the ‘Command’, ‘Shift’ and ‘4’ keys together again. Once the selection box appears, press the ‘Control’ key and the screenshot will be copied to the clipboard. You can then paste the image into a word document or a presentation slide.
