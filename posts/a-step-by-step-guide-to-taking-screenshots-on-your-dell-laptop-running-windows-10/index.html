1.Overview of Taking Screenshots in Windows 10#

Capturing a screenshot is a great way to save important information, share your screen with others, or just document what you are working on. In this article, we will walk you through the steps to take a screenshot on your Dell laptop running Windows 10.

First, you need to decide whether you want to take a full-screen screenshot or capture a certain part of the screen. If you want to capture your entire screen, simply press the Windows logo key and “PrtScn” (or “Print Screen”) on your keyboard at the same time. If you want to capture only a particular part of the screen, press the Windows logo key and “Shift” and “S” at the same time. This will open a tool called the “Snipping Tool”, which you can use to select any part of your screen that you would like to capture.

Once you have taken the screenshot, Windows 10 will save it to the “Screenshots” folder in your Pictures library. To access this folder, simply open the “File Explorer” and navigate to the “Pictures” folder. Here, you can view the screenshot you just captured, save it to a different location, or share it with others.

Finally, if you don’t want to use the built-in screenshot tools provided by Windows 10, you can always download third-party screenshot apps. There are many great options out there that are simple and easy to use, and they offer more features like editing, annotating, and sharing your screenshots.

No matter which method you choose, taking a screenshot on your Dell laptop running Windows 10 is easy and straightforward. Follow the steps above, and you’ll be taking screenshots like a pro in no time!

A2. You can access the Windows Snipping Tool by clicking on the Start menu, then type “Snipping Tool” into the search bar. You can also type “Snip & Sketch” which will take you to the same place. The Snipping Tool is located within the Windows accessories folder.
