- Steps to Connect External Microphone to Android Device#

Recording professional audio on an Android device is possible with the use of an external microphone. This article provides the steps to connect an external microphone to an Android device.

First, you will need to ensure you have the right type of connector. Depending on your device, you might need an adapter or connector. Common microphone connectors include 3.5mm, USB, and Lightning.

Then, connect the microphone to the correct port on the Android device. If you are using a 3.5mm microphone, plug it into the headphone jack on the device. For USB and Lightning connectors, plug the microphone into the USB-C or Lightning port.

Once you have connected your microphone to your device, you now need to set up the audio source. You can do this by opening your settings app and navigating to the audio settings. Here, select your microphone as the primary audio input source.

Finally, you can now start to record your audio. To do this, open an audio recording app and select your external microphone as your input source. Once you have set everything up, you can now begin recording professional audio on your Android device.
