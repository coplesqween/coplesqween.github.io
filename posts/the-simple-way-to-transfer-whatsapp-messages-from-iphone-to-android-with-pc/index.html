- Steps to Transfer Whatsapp Messages from iPhone to Android Using PC#

Transferring Whatsapp Messages from iPhone to Android now made easy! With just a few simple steps, you can now make the switch from iPhone to Android seamlessly. All you need is a PC to make the transfer. Here are the steps to follow:

First, you need to back up your Whatsapp data from your iPhone. To do that, open your Whatsapp and head to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup > Back Up Now. Once the backup is complete, connect your iPhone to your PC and then copy the Whatsapp backup file to your PC.

Next, you need to transfer the Whatsapp backup file from the PC to your Android device. To do this, connect your Android phone to the PC and then transfer the Whatsapp backup file to the internal storage of your device.

Finally, open Whatsapp on your Android device and head to Settings > Chats > Chat Backup > Restore. The Whatsapp backup file from the iPhone should now be restored on your Android device.

That’s it! You’ve successfully transferred your Whatsapp messages from iPhone to Android with the help of your PC. Now you can start enjoying your new Android device!
