Next, you will need to open your iPhone’s Settings and select Contacts. From this menu, select Accounts. On this screen, you will need to select your Google account and enable the Contacts setting. Finally, you can also use iCloud to transfer contacts from Android to iPhone. First, open the Settings application on your iPhone and select iCloud. Here, you can select the Contacts option and then turn on iCloud backup. Your Android contacts will now be stored in iCloud and can be synced to your new iPhone.
