1. How to turn off the sound when a screenshot is taken on iPad Air 4#

Taking screenshots on iPad Air 4 is a great way to capture a moment or an activity for any purpose. However, you may want to turn off the sound when you take the screenshots. The good news is you can easily disable it! Here’s a step-by-step guide on how to turn off the sound when a screenshot is taken on iPad Air 4.

First, open the Settings app on your iPad Air 4. Then, tap on Sounds & Haptics. Then you can find the Screenshot toggle button at the bottom. Tap on it to toggle it to Off position. Now you should no longer hear a sound when you take a screenshot.

If you want to turn the sound back on, simply retrace the steps above and toggle the Screenshot switch to the On position. You will hear the familiar sound when you take a screenshot.
