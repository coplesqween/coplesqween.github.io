Q2: How do I take Steam Screenshots?

A2: Taking Steam Screenshots is easy. Simply press the ‘F12’ key while playing a game and the screenshot will be taken. You can also press the ‘Print Screen’ key to take screenshots.

Q3: Where do Steam Screenshots get stored?

A3: Steam Screenshots are stored in your Steam installation folder, usually located at C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\userdata[user id]\760\remote[app id]\screenshots.

Q4: How can I share my Steam Screenshots?

A4: You can share your Steam Screenshots via the Steam Community. To do so, open the Steam overlay while in a game (Shift+Tab). Then, navigate to the ‘Screenshots’ section, select the screenshot you want to share, and click ‘Share’.
