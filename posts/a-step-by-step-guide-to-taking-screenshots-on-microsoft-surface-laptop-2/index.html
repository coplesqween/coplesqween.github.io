Q. What is a screenshot?

A. A screenshot is an image of your computer’s desktop or an open window. It allows you to capture and share what you see on your screen with others.

Q. How do I take a screenshot on a Microsoft Surface Laptop 2?

A. To take a screenshot on your Microsoft Surface Laptop 2, press and hold the Windows button and Volume Down button simultaneously until you hear a camera shutter sound. The captured screenshot will automatically save in the Screenshots folder located in the Pictures folder.

Q.What image file types can I capture with my Microsoft Surface Laptop 2?

A. Your Microsoft Surface Laptop 2 will automatically capture screenshots in the PNG file format.

Q. Is there an easier way to take screenshots on my Microsoft Surface Laptop 2?

A. Yes, you can also use the shortcut key combination of Windows + Shift + S to capture screenshots on your Microsoft Surface Laptop 2. This shortcut will open the Snip & Sketch tool which allows you to easily capture and alter your screenshots.

Q. Where can I find my screenshots once I take them?

A. All the screenshots you take on your Microsoft Surface Laptop 2 will be saved in the Screenshots folder, located in the Pictures folder. You can easily find this folder by typing “Screenshots” in the search bar of your Windows Start menu.
