1. Steps to Take a Screenshot on a Lenovo Yoga Windows Device#

Taking screenshots on a Lenovo Yoga device with Windows operating system can be a great way to capture images of important files, webpages, or other items that you need to save. To help you do so, here are the steps you need to take:

  1. Press the Windows and Volume down buttons at the same time.
  2. The screen will dim for a moment and the screenshot will be saved in the screenshots folder, which you can find in the Pictures folder.
  3. If you want to open or share the screenshot right away, press the Windows Key + Print Screen.
  4. The screenshot will be saved in the Screenshots folder and automatically opened in the Photos app.
