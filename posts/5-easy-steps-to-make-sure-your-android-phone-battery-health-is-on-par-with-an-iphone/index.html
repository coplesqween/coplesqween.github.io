Do you want to make sure your Android phone’s battery health is as good as an iPhone’s? It might seem like a daunting task, but don’t worry!   1. Get an app to monitor your battery usage: With the help of a battery usage-monitoring app, you can keep track of the apps that consume the most power and reduce your usage of them.

  2. Charge only when needed: It is very important to keep your battery from overcharging. So, only charge your phone when you need it and unplug it when it is fully charged.

  5. Replace your battery as needed: Replacing your battery every two years or so is an essential step to keep your phone running as if it were brand new. Doing this ensures that your battery will be able to keep up with the latest technologies and maintain your phone’s performance.

These five steps provide a simple and straightforward way to make your Android phone battery health equal to that of an iPhone. Following these steps can ensure a long and healthy battery life for your phone.

  4. Open the Settings app and navigate to the Developer Options page. Here you will be able to adjust the battery settings of your phone, as well as settings for managing the CPU, RAM, and GPU resources.

Following these five easy steps will help you make sure your Android phone battery health is on par with an iPhone. This will ensure that your phone runs at its best and will last you for many years to come.

5. How to Extend Battery Life#
